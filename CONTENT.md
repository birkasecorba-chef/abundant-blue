## Content Strategy Notes

### Voice & Tone Principles

1. **Obsessive but not unhinged** — Dedicated, not crazy
2. **Technical precision** — Use exact terminology  
3. **Narrative tension** — Will we find it? When?
4. **Respectful devotion** — This jacket matters
5. **Systematic documentation** — Every detail recorded

### Content Updates

**Weekly:** Platform status updates, new near matches
**Monthly:** Search methodology refinements, statistics analysis  
**Quarterly:** Network expansion announcements, progress reports
**When found:** Victory documentation and hunt retrospective

### Tone Examples

**Good:** "Systematic search across authenticated platforms"
**Bad:** "Looking everywhere for this jacket"

**Good:** "Day 47: Algorithm detected false positive on platform 12"  
**Bad:** "Checked eBay again, no luck"

**Good:** "Style 84684 in colorway ABDB remains elusive"
**Bad:** "Still can't find that blue jacket"

The copy should feel like reading the field notes of a devoted researcher, not the rants of someone who lost their favorite jacket. Every word serves the narrative of thoughtful, methodical pursuit of a specific and meaningful object.

---

**This content strategy transforms a product search into a story worth following. Each piece of copy reinforces the commitment, precision, and slight beautiful madness of the hunt.**
